Frequently Asked Questions

?Does this export a 3D model file?

This page is a live WebGL preview for exploration. Downloadable mesh export is not included yet.

?Why does my SVG look empty?

Very complex paths, embedded raster images only, or missing fills may not extrude. Try a simpler icon SVG.

?Is my SVG uploaded to a server?

No. The SVG is read in your browser and processed with WebGL; it is not stored by Toolker.

?What powers the 3D pipeline?

Three.js extrusion from SVG paths. Geometry concepts are adapted from the MIT-licensed 3dsvg project—see NOTICE in the repository.
