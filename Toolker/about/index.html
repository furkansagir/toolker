About Toolker

Simple online tools for files and text

Toolker is an online collection of utilities for everyday work with documents and media. You will find PDF tools (merge, compress, convert, and more), image tools (resize, compress, convert formats), and text helpers (word count, slug and case conversion, and small generators)—all in one place.

We focus on a straightforward experience: open a tool, upload or paste your content, and download or copy the result. There is nothing to install, and core workflows are free so you can get things done quickly without jumping between apps.

Your trust matters to us. Where a tool runs entirely in your browser, your files are not uploaded to Toolker for storage; they are handled on your device. For details on how we treat data, see our Privacy Policy.

Privacy in plain words

We design Toolker so you can work confidently. For typical file tools, files are not stored after processing, using temporary processing in your browser. A few features may talk to outside services (for example live currency rates) without storing your documents. The full picture is in our Privacy Policy.
