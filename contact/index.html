Have a question about a tool, noticed something that looks wrong, or want to share feedback? Send us a message—we read what comes in and use it to improve Toolker.

Email: support@toolker.com

Please include enough detail for us to help: which tool you used, what you expected, and what happened instead. We cannot troubleshoot account issues—we do not run sign-ups— but we can point you to the FAQ or Privacy Policy when relevant.

For legal or privacy-specific requests, mention “Privacy” or “Legal” in the subject line so we can route your email appropriately.
